Welcome to FlickrNav

Explore an interactive map visualization of photographs shared on Flickr. Use the map below to interact with clusters, discover individual photos, and uncover trends in photography worldwide.

Here are some tips to get you started:

  • Zoom in to explore photo clusters at a more granular level.
  • Click on cluster marker bubbles to reveal individual photos in the area represented by the cluster marker.
  • Click on photo bubbles to view the full photo thumbnail and additional details.
  • The photo gallery beneath the map explorer displays a selection of photos contained within the currently visible map area.
Color Legend for Cluster Marker Density

    Green: 1-9 photos

    Yellow: 10-99 photos

    Orange: 100+ photos

Acknowledging Indigenous Histories and Contributions

The geotagged historical photographs presented in this project often depict locations with rich and complex histories that predate colonial narratives. We recognize that many of these places are situated on lands stewarded by Indigenous peoples for thousands of years. These lands hold cultural, spiritual, and historical significance that may not be fully visible in the colonial-era photographs featured in the Flickr Commons archive.

To honor these histories and perspectives, we propose the following steps for the next stage of the project:

  • Including Land Acknowledgments: Where possible, integrate land acknowledgments into the interface, recognizing the Indigenous peoples whose lands are represented in the photographs.
  • Highlighting Indigenous Narratives: Explore opportunities to include metadata or supplementary information that provides Indigenous context to the photos or regions displayed.
